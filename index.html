About Verve

About Verve

NIT Durgapur's annual youth cum cultural fest, Verve is the largest of its kind in Eastern India, with thousands of participants engaged in various fun activities throughout the 3 days that it is conducted. It has Star Talk as its main attraction, with various admirable personalities sharing their life experiences and journeys with our students.
